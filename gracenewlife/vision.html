Vision Statement

Connecting Communities with Christ

  • Jesus Christ & The Bible - We are a Bible-based Christian church. The person of Jesus Christ is central to all we do and through Him we have relationship with God the Father and the Holy Spirit. Committed to the whole Bible as the Word of God and our final authority and foundation for rule and practice in our lives, family and community.
  • The Ministry of the Holy Spirit - We are Spirit-filled church. We believe that each Christian should develop the fruit of the Spirit and seek to use the gifts of the Spirit for the benefit of others. Being the followers of Jesus Christ, continually growing making others His disciples.
  • Prayer - we are a praying church. Our goal is to become a house of prayer for all nations. Raising prayer partners to intercede for our country and nations.
  • Praise & Worship - we are a worshipping church. Our goal is to express our devotion to God through joyful praise and intimate worship.
  • Love & Care - we are a loving church. Our goal is to see every person loved and cared for in such a way that they feel accepted, valued and have a sense of belonging. Our goal is to see every member developing their God-given abilities gifts and using them to serve others.
  • Outreach & evangelism - we are an evangelistic church. Our goal is to reach lost people both near and far and influence our community for righteousness through evangelism, community outreach, missions and church planting.
  • Unity & Diversity- we are a unified Body of Christ. Our goal is to bridge racial, cultural, gender, socio-economic and generational gaps so as to create a community where all people can relate together in a spirit of love and unity.
