We believe that in the person of Jesus Christ the Divine and human natures are united, so that He is truly and properly God and man.

We believe that the Scriptures of the 66 Books were given by inspiration of God, and that they only constitute the Divine rule of Christian faith and practice.

We believe in trinity - the Father, the Son, and the Holy spirit, undivided in essence and co-equal in power and glory.

Our mission is to raise up to be the kind of people God wants us to be and fulfill the vision of God to make fervent disciples of all nations for Jesus Christ, who will reach out and impact communities, cities and nations for the kingdom of God.
