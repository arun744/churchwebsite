Gideon P Jesudoss is the Senior Minister of Grace New Life Church, a diverse community of Christians meeting to the Glory of Jesus Christ in St. Marys NSW Australia. Gideon has a genuine love for people and a passion to help them grow and live for Christ. He has wide experience of ministry for over 20 years with theological studies, degree from Madras University (India), with Diploma in management and Chaplaincy from Australian Institutions. Gideon is happily married to Jemy and they have two teen children; Grace and Glady Aaron.
